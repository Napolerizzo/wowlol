Hello, I am Mosaic MUN 2.0 NEW

Mosaic Model United Nations is an inter-school conference engineered for students who think with precision and argue with conviction. Organized by Duer.in and hosted in Faridabad, Mosaic is not a participation exercise. It is a competitive intellectual proving ground.

Delegates engage across four distinct simulation formats. Edition 2.0 builds on four years of precedent and over five hundred alumni. Selection is intentional. Standards are non-negotiable.
